Introduction

In times of rapid scientific progress and a constant influx of new technologies, the digital landscape of scientific research can feel overwhelming. That's why efficient information retrieval is crucial here. The European Open Science Cloud (EOSC) Marketplace has emerged as a valuable tool for users seeking to tap into a diverse range of resources.

In this article, we will explore how to effectively navigate the EOSC Marketplace and leverage its advanced search to discover the forefront research assets.

At its core, the EOSC Marketplace is a user-friendly platform designed to simplify the process of finding relevant resources. Whether you're a seasoned researcher or a novice, the platform's intuitive interface ensures that you can access the information you need with ease.

Types of research products offered in the EOSC Catalog & Marketplace

Publication - formal, peer-reviewed record in journals or proceedings, contributing new knowledge to EOSC, sourced from various paper repositories.

Data - In EOSC, it includes all digital research outputs—datasets, metadata, publications, and software code. This forms the foundation for analysis, interpretation, and generating new knowledge in data-driven scientific work.

Software - Instructions for computer tasks in EOSC—aiding researchers in experiments, simulations, and data analysis. Stored in digital files or repositories for easy access.

Going Beyond the Basics

While the basic search is a good starting point, the real potential of the EOSC Marketplace lies in its advanced search capabilities. Using this feature expands the possibilities for refining your search.

The EOSC Marketplace is designed to provide a comprehensive search experience. It consists of three key elements:

Basic Search - always positioned at the top of the webpage, this is where you can enter your search queries.

Advanced Search - adjacent to the main search bar, the advanced search feature allows you to refine your search using specific criteria.

Standard Filters - on the left side of the webpage, you'll find traditional filters that help you narrow down your search results.

With the advanced search, you can filter your results based on specific criteria such as resource type, keywords, title, author, and additional search operators, including 'and' and 'or.' The subsequent operators allow you to refine your search even further by not only specifying exact phrases but also excluding specific terms. To put it simply, the advanced search enables you to find resources with much greater precision.

Streamlining Your Search

While exploring our search engine capabilities, you'll also come across standard filters that streamline your search results even more. These filters offer conventional choices, such as scientific domain, access type, language, and more. With a simple checkmark, you can instantly group or categorize your search results by these criteria.

As you utilize the advance search, think of it as your trusty research companion, helping you navigate the rich world of repositories and scientific materials.

Conclusion

To sum it up, the EOSC Marketplace is a user-friendly platform that offers a comprehensive search experience. Its basic search, advanced search, and standard filters work harmoniously, so researchers can locate and access resources essential to their work.
